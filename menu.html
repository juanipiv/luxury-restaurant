    At Canma, the menu is not merely a list of dishes; it is a curated narrative of transformation. Each course represents a precise study in molecular interaction, designed to challenge the palate and awaken the senses.

    Guided by Chef Yuan Vîlchèz, our culinary program moves beyond traditional gastronomy. We source the finest global ingredients and reimagine their essence through scientific rigor and artistic expression. From ephemeral foams to crystallized aromas, every plate is an edible sculpture that defies expectation.

    We invite you to explore our tasting menus, a structured journey through the unexpected, where innovation meets the purest definition of flavor.

    Tasting Menus

    The Vîlchèz Constellation

    A structured 8-course journey through the architectural and elemental frontiers of modern cuisine.

    I. Initialization

    The Ephemeral Cube

    A foundational statement of precision. The geometric lattice offers a singular bite of rich terrine.

    II. Ethereal Phase

    Orbital Purity

    Structural spheres (milk mousse) suspended in a fragrant liquid medium with molecular pearls. This light composition underscores the ethereal possibilities of flavor.

    The Distilled Ocean

    A single, ultra-concentrated capsule of deep-sea reduction, flash-frozen using liquid nitrogen. Designed for one sip, culminating in a citrus vapor cloud to cleanse the palate.

    III. Dimensionality & Earth

    Dimensional Triplet

    A signature composition of geometric progression: three caramelized cubes on a savoury lattice.

    The Gravitational Study

    A signature composition of vertical structure: aged protein (Wagyu/venison) and seared foie gras.

    IV. Epilogue

    The Petal of Ascent

    A delicate spherified sheet of crystallized basil, filled with pressurized olive oil foam. A transitional course designed to clear the richness and prepare the palate for sweetness.

    Chromic Disruption

    A structural sphere of dark chocolate mousse that shatters upon light impact. It reveals a liquid passion fruit core, a final expression of controlled chaos.

    Deconstructed Coffee & Petit Fours

    A series of small, concentrated experiences including caramelized coffee gelée. The finale features compressed hazelnut air and a single crystal of sea salt.

    The Alchemist's Transmutation

    An immersive 8-course study into the transformative power of heat, cold, and molecular interaction, guided by the principles of culinary alchemy.

    I. Initialization

    The Amber Drop

    A single, warm spherification of smoked paprika consommé, presented on a dehydrated rice chip. A deliberate burst of temperature contrast and intense flavor upon contact.

    II. Elemental Shift

    Sea Foam & Petal Gel

    Aerated Atlantic essence (sea foam) paired with a translucent rose petal gel. A study in ephemeral texture and olfactory purity that vanishes on the palate.

    Frozen Basil Dust

    Nitrogen-blasted basil powder and crystallized lemon air, served over frozen sheep's milk yogurt. A sudden thermal shock designed for immediate aromatic release.

    III. Transmutation

    Clarity of Dashi

    A slow-cooked cube of kingfish, gently infused with smoked Kombu dashi. Sealed under a cloche to capture its aromatic essence and visual simplicity

    Compressed Quail & Ash

    Compressed quail breast glazed in a black garlic reduction, dusted with onion ash. A powerful earthy flavor contrasted by sharp pickled berries and texture.

    Golden Beet Lattice

    Thin slices of golden beet formed into a perfect lattice structure. Topped with mustard seed caviar and a smoked goat cheese emulsion for complexity.

    IV. Finale & Resolution

    Mint Suspension

    A perfectly clear mint consommé with floating cubes of suspended sugar gel. A transitional liquid that cleanses the palate and recalibrates the senses.

    The Chocolate Singularity

    A single sphere of 70% dark chocolate shell filled with warm olive oil ganache. Designed for a shattering effect and temperature collision to conclude the journey.

    Price: 330€ per person.

    Optional Wine Pairing (Maridaje): 180€

    Signature Dishes

    The Gravitational Study

    One of Chef Vîlchèz's signature compositions, this dish explores vertical structure and primal indulgence. It centers on low-temperature aged protein (Wagyu beef or venison) and seared foie gras, founded upon a savoury raviolo and wilted greens.

    The structure is elevated by aerated fungi rosettes (truffle mousse) and dehydrated root filaments, unified by a deep Bordeaux reduction. This balance of primal richness and molecular contrast delivers an unprecedented culinary experience.

    The Ephemeral Cube

    Recognized as one of Canma's foundational statements, this dish embodies the philosophy of precision and minimalism. It presents a perfectly geometric lattice supporting a rich, savory terrine and a gentle white emulsion. The focal point is a sculpted chlorophyll essence that rests in intentional opposition to the square form.

    This singular bite is a masterclass in controlled texture and instantaneous flavor release, designed to initiate the molecular journey with a single, flawless moment.

    Dimensional Triplet

    This signature composition is a study in geometric progression. Three perfectly caramelized cubes of slow-cooked protein (e.g., Iberico pork belly or high-grade eel) are presented upon a dehydrated savoury lattice. The central theme is the molecular glaze—a reduction that seals in complex umami notes. The foundation is layered with finely shredded pickled root to introduce a sharp, acidic contrast.

    This is an exercise in sequential tasting, where the flavor intensifies with each component, revealing the inherent beauty in structural repetition.

    Orbital Purity

    Serving as a masterclass in controlled form, this dish contrasts with the heavy elements of the menu through absolute lightness. It features several structural spheres (a mousse of fresh sheep's milk or high-grade yogurt) suspended in a fragrant liquid medium (clear citrus reduction). The surface is dotted with molecular pearls—a delicate caviar that provides a burst of focused flavor upon contact.

    Garnished with edible petals, Orbital Purity delivers a weightless sensory experience, highlighting the ethereal possibilities of flavor and structure.
