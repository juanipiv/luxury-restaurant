    The New Dimension of Flavor. ~~ Yuan Vîlchêz

    About Us

    (01)

    The story of Canma does not begin in a kitchen, but in the mind of its founder, Yuan Vîlchèz. Before he was a chef, Vîlchèz was a student of physics and chemistry, fascinated not by food itself, but by the structure of flavor: the molecules that compose an aroma, the geometry of a salt crystal, the physics of a perfect emulsion.

    He saw gastronomy as a science that had not yet found its artist. For him, an ingredient was not an end, but a set of possibilities—a code waiting to be rewritten.

    (02)

    For years, Yuan Vîlchèz vanished from traditional culinary circles. He retreated to a small laboratory, armed with rotary evaporators, liquid nitrogen, and a botanical library. His obsession was singular: deconstruction. He wasn't just breaking down food; he was isolating the very molecules of memory.

    Could he capture the essence of summer rain on a plate? Could a diner taste the texture of a sound? He spent a decade breaking rules in search of a purer truth, learning to control emotion through science. He learned to distill smoke, solidify air, and transform a simple vegetable into a dozen unrecognizable forms that tasted, unmistakably, of their origin.

    (03)

    When Vîlchèz was ready, he did not open a restaurant. He opened Canma.

    The name is his manifesto: a fusion of "Canvas" and "Materia" (Matter). A blank canvas where raw material is elevated into an art form. Canma was designed without distraction—a space of minimalist luxury where the only protagonist is the experience happening on the table. The venue is a gallery, and each dish, an ephemeral masterpiece.

    (04)

    The world of fine dining took notice. The three Michelin stars were not a goal, but the inevitable consequence of an absolute devotion to innovation. Today, Canma is not just a restaurant; it is the stage where Chef Yuan Vîlchèz and his team present their ongoing research and culinary discoveries every night. Guests are invited to participate in a symphony of flavors, aromas, and textures previously unimagined.

    A dinner at Canma is not just a meal. It is an invitation to question everything you thought you knew about flavor, texture, and form. It is a multi-sensory journey guided by the visionary hand of a master who treats science with the soul of a poet, revealing the hidden universe within each ingredient.

    The Callibrated Cellar

    Our wine program extends the Chef's vision into the glass. Curated by our Head Sommelier, the cellar features rare and exceptional vintages sourced from unconventional global vineyards and historic cellars. The physical environment is a controlled sanctuary, preserving the molecular integrity of every bottle.

    The approach is rooted in chemical compatibility, establishing a profound molecular dialogue between the liquid and the plate. Discover pairings that transcend tradition, designed to elevate the scientific artistry of every course.
