    The singular vision of Canma is brought to life by a collective of exceptionally talented individuals. More than a kitchen brigade, our team operates at the precise intersection of culinary art and scientific research.

    Under the exacting direction of Chef Yuan Vîlchèz, this group of chefs, researchers, sommeliers, and hospitality specialists works in perfect unison. Each member is a master of their craft, driven by a meticulous attention to detail and a shared, unwavering passion for innovation.

    Together, we are dedicated to executing a flawless, multi-sensory journey for every guest.

    Our Leadership

    Yuan Vîlchèz

    Founder & Executive Chef

    The visionary behind Canma, Chef Vîlchèz combines his profound understanding of physics and chemistry with an artist's soul.

    His relentless pursuit of flavor deconstruction and sensory innovation has earned Canma three Michelin stars, cementing his reputation as a pioneer in molecular gastronomy. He personally oversees every aspect of the culinary journey.

    Elena Sato

    Restaurant Manager

    The meticulous architect of Canma's front-of-house operations, ensuring every guest experience is seamless and exceptional.

    With years of luxury hospitality management, they orchestrate service with unparalleled grace and precision.

    The Culinary Team

    Bruno Lobardi

    Head Chef

    Working in direct collaboration with Chef Vîlchèz, the Head Chef translates innovative concepts into daily culinary execution.

    Their expertise in molecular techniques and ingredient mastery is fundamental to maintaining Canma's exacting standards.

    Augusto De Luca

    Sous Chef

    Integral to the kitchen's rhythm, our Sous Chefs lead specialized stations, ensuring every component of a dish meets Canma's rigorous quality and artistic vision.

    Their precision and leadership are vital to the team's success.

    Service & Wine Program

    Sebastian Valmont

    Head Sommelier

    Curating Canma's award-winning wine cellar, our Head Sommelier expertly pairs each dish's complex flavors with rare and exceptional vintages, guiding guests through a complementary sensory exploration.

    Their philosophy seeks a molecular dialogue between glass and plate. By selecting bottles with distinct chemical profiles, they ensure every sip elevates the scientific artistry of the cuisine.

    Marco Vianello

    Maître d'Hôtel

    The first point of contact and an ambassador of Canma, the Maître d'Hôtel ensures a warm, sophisticated welcome and orchestrates the dining room experience with intuitive service and personalized attention.

    Mastering the art of anticipation, they create a seamless environment where time seems to suspend. This ensures that the focus remains entirely on the unfolding narrative of the tasting menu without distraction.
